ABOUT

LILIAN OLUBI

She is a Finance and investment profess-
ional with over twenty years of Capital
Markets experience. She is currently the
CEO of a leading Investment services firm
in Nigeria and has had a diverse career
in a few leading institutions at different
life cycles with a track record of driving
growth. A woman of many parts, Lilian is
enthusiastic about public service and
civic responsibility and is a firm
advocate for social enterprises
and SMEs as the engine house
of a strong economy.

She founded The Gold Lilies Global
Services, a social venture conglomerate
providing value across different sectors.
She has been a boardroom judge for the
past seven seasons of the successful
entrepreneurship reality TV show – ‘The
Next Titan’ which promotes enterprise
among youths by pruning their business
ideas, sharpening acumen and providing
business capital. She sits on several
boards in the corporate and social
sector and is the co-founder of Drug
Aid Africa Initiative.

“Osamede, the Stage Play” which is
her debut project in the arts is a tool
she intends to utilize as an avenue to
raise the crucial topics of human rights,
women’s role in social change, creation of
job opportunities for youths and to promote
the creative arts industry in Nigeria with
the strength of her corporate background.
Lilian is a Chartered Stockbroker and
Authorized Dealing Clerk of the Nigerian
Stock Exchange, she holds a Bsc in Business
Administration from the University of Benin
and a Masters of leadership in Development
Finance from the Frankfurt school of Finance &
Management, Germany. She is also an author
and is happily married and blessed with
two lovely children.

OLATUNDE ODUWOLE

He is a Fellow of The Institute of
Chartered Accountants of NIgeria (ICAN)
and an International Associate of the of
Science degree in Banking and Finance from
Boston University, Massachusetts USA, with
over 25 years work experience in commercial
finance, corporate strategy, business
development, audit, tax and accounting
in the oil and gas industry, audit
and management consulting across
Africa, Europe and North America.

He is the Head of Finance, Oil and Gas
Projects, Shell Petroleum Development
Company. Nig. Ltd and until recently,
the Finance Director of Shell Nigeria
Gas Limited and Director on the Board
of West Africa Gas Pipeline Company
Limited (WAPCO). He has led several
complex multi-billion dollars commercial
transactions in the oil and gas sector
in Nigeria, including the recently
completed financing for Nigeria LNG
(NLNG) Train 7 expansion program.

Tunde is an adventurous traveller,
he enjoys playing the saxophone,
reading, and theatre production.
He produced the highly acclaimed
play “Gula on Stage” in 2017 a
riveting story based on President
Obasanjo’s prison experience.
He is happily married to Dr. Jumoke
Oduwole and they are blessed
with two lovely children.

AYO AJAYI

AYO AJAYI / Director / he is a theatre
producer and director is a pioneer music
director in the Nigerian theatre industry
who to date has directed well over 50 musicals
producing some of Nigeria's finests. His active
participation in the Nigerian theatre space
started when he directed the music of the
popular Saro the musical and Wakaa the
musical (Lagos & London) both from
the stables of BAP Production.

His musical talent was honed from a
very early age with lessons from his
father before been nurtured at the CMS
Grammar School where he eventually became
the School Choral director and pianist.
His debut was at the age of seven when
He performed at the Economic Community of
West African States (ECOWAS) conference
concert at the Sheraton Hotels in Abuja
with the group Rising Star led by Tosin
Adejumo and he has since then been
involved in several educational and
performance tours both in and
outside the country.

Ayo Ajayi is the Creative Director of
Declassical Arts & Entertainment, a top
production company in Nigeria which
has produced phenomenal musical theatre
performances like OMG the Musical, Legends
the Musical, The Lost Chord, Fela; Arrest
the Music, Gula’, Ajala, Home the Musical,
Ununcha the musical, to mention but a few.
He was also the Music Director for Midnight
Hotel, Wait, Evolution of African Music,
Full Circle, 'Obele and The Storyteller'.
Ayo, who has taught music in schools for
more than two decades is the immediate
past President of the MUSON School
Alumni Association and is also a
Pianist, Organist,Composer, and
Choral Conductor.

Gold Lilies Global Services was founded
in 2017 in Lagos, Nigeria as a social impact
outfit for diverse causes. It has gone ahead
to be the funding instrument and corporate
representation for both philanthropic and
commercial ventures amongst other initiatives.
Inspired by its overarching objectives to
promote excellence and humanity, the vision
of the entity and all its expressions is
simple – Bringing inspiration and
empowerment opportunities, to be
a lighthouse to help create
better human experiences.
